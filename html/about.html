Founder

Angela Basset (1947-2017)

        Angela Basset was born on the 25th of July 1947 in Colchester, England. She is one of the pioneer advocates for animal rights in the UK. She has always been an animal lover as she always strived for the safe care of animals. She established Angie's Pet Store in her later years, to make sure she is able to take as many animals off the streets as she could. She sadly passed away after a brief illness in her sleep surrounded by her loving animals that she had taken care of for years. The company is now run by her great granddaughter Genevieve Clark.
